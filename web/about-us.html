Nexus Info

Information
Last Updated On 2-Nov-2023

The SCP Foundation is a clandestine organization tasked with the containment, protection, and study of anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena. The Foundation operates across the globe from a network of top-secret sites, each designed to contain and analyze different types of SCPs. However, one site in particular stands out as the most dangerous and challenging to manage- Site Nexus. Located in an undisclosed location, Site Nexus is a sprawling complex that houses some of the most powerful and unpredictable SCPs in Foundation custody. Site Nexus was built in response to a series of escalating incidents involving SCPs with reality-bending abilities. The Foundation recognized the need for a site capable of containing these entities, and Site Nexus was born. However, it quickly became clear that this was no ordinary site. The SCPs held at Site Nexus include reality-warping entities, extradimensional horrors, and even some SCPs that defy classification. The containment procedures for these anomalies are complex and ever-changing, as the SCPs themselves can influence reality and disrupt normal operations. The staff at Site Nexus are some of the most experienced and skilled in the Foundation, but even they are constantly on edge. The site has suffered numerous containment breaches over the years, resulting in catastrophic loss of life and property damage.
